What We Do

Complete Roofing Services

Residential, commercial, storm damage, and everything in between — all done by our own certified crew, backed by a lifetime guarantee.

Residential

Full Roof Replacement

When repairs are no longer cost-effective — or when damage is extensive enough that a full replacement gives you better long-term value — Summit Roofing handles the complete job from tear-off to final inspection. We remove all existing roofing materials, inspect and repair the decking, and install a new roofing system built for Colorado's climate.

We work with IKO, GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed products. As IKO Diamond Preferred contractors, we can offer factory-extended warranties up to 50 years on qualifying systems. Every replacement includes a free post-install inspection and photos of every stage.

  • Full tear-off & deck inspection
  • Structural deck repairs included when needed
  • IKO, GAF, Owens Corning, CertainTeed systems
  • Manufacturer warranties up to 50 years
  • Lifetime workmanship warranty (transferable)
  • Post-install drone inspection & photo report
Repair

Roof Repair & Emergency Service

Leaks don't wait for business hours. Our repair team handles emergency calls 24/7, with same-day response for active leaks and next-day scheduling for urgent (but not emergency) repairs. We diagnose the source of the problem — not just patch the symptom — and provide a written repair report with photos.

Common repairs include blown-off shingles, failed flashing at chimneys and skylights, cracked ridge caps, ice dam damage, and valley deterioration. If we identify issues during a repair visit that suggest a replacement is coming, we'll tell you honestly — we won't oversell a band-aid if a bigger solution is warranted.

  • 24/7 emergency leak response
  • Same-day repair for active leaks
  • Chimney & skylight flashing repair
  • Ridge cap & valley repair
  • Written report with before/after photos
  • All repairs warrantied for 3–5 years
Storm

Hail & Storm Damage Restoration

Colorado averages 9–11 major hail events per year. Denver's "Hail Alley" makes it one of the most hail-prone cities in North America. We specialize in hail damage assessment, documentation, and restoration — and we've helped thousands of Denver homeowners recover covered claims from their insurance companies.

Our process: free inspection → detailed damage report with drone photography → insurance claim support → approved scope of work → restoration. Our full-time insurance claims coordinator works alongside your adjuster to ensure nothing is missed and your claim is approved for the full scope of damage.

  • Free hail damage inspection & assessment
  • Drone photography & documented damage report
  • Insurance claim coordination from start to finish
  • Work with all major insurers (State Farm, Allstate, USAA, etc.)
  • Certified hail-resistant material options
  • Class 4 impact-resistant shingle upgrades available
Commercial

Commercial & Multi-Family Roofing

Commercial roofing requires different expertise, different materials, and a team that understands how to work around active business operations. Summit Roofing's commercial division handles TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, metal standing seam, and built-up roofing systems for warehouses, retail centers, office buildings, and multi-family properties.

We provide pre-project planning to minimize disruption, after-hours and weekend scheduling when required, and a dedicated commercial project manager on-site throughout installation. All commercial work is permitted, inspected, and backed by a 20-year workmanship warranty.

  • TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen & metal systems
  • Warehouses, retail, office & multi-family
  • Minimal disruption scheduling (nights & weekends)
  • Dedicated commercial project manager
  • Permit management & code compliance
  • 20-year commercial workmanship warranty
Inspection

Professional Roof Inspections

Our comprehensive roof inspections cover the full system — shingles, flashing, ridge and hip caps, gutters, downspouts, attic ventilation, and moisture evaluation. We use drone photography on every inspection so you can see exactly what we're seeing, and we provide a detailed written report the same day.

We offer three inspection types: pre-purchase inspections for homebuyers, post-storm inspections for insurance claims, and annual maintenance inspections for homeowners who want to stay ahead of issues. Free inspections are available for homeowners considering a repair or replacement.

  • Drone photography on every inspection
  • Full attic moisture & ventilation evaluation
  • Written report with photo documentation
  • Pre-purchase & pre-sale inspections
  • Post-storm damage assessment
  • Free inspection with any repair or replacement inquiry
Protection

Gutters, Downspouts & Snow Guards

A great roof is undermined by a failing gutter system. We install seamless K-style and half-round aluminum gutters in any length, eliminating the weak joints that cause leaks. Our gutter systems are properly sloped, anchored to structural rafter tails, and include hidden fasteners for a cleaner finished look.

For Denver's heavy snowpack, we install snow guards and snow retention systems on metal roofs, slate, and steep-pitch shingles to prevent dangerous ice dam avalanches. We also install heat cable systems in valleys and gutters for homes with chronic ice dam problems.

  • Seamless aluminum gutters in 20+ colors
  • K-style and half-round profiles available
  • Gutter guard & leaf protection systems
  • Snow guard & retention bar installation
  • Ice dam prevention heat cable systems
  • Downspout extension & drainage solutions
